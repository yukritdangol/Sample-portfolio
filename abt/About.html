Your Name

Your Name is a 2016 Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai that tells the story of two teenagers, Taki Tachibana and Mitsuha Miyamizu, who mysteriously begin switching bodies despite living in different parts of Japan. Mitsuha, a girl from the rural town of Itomori, dreams of escaping her quiet life for the bustling excitement of Tokyo. Taki, a high school boy in Tokyo, lives a busy life juggling his studies, part-time job, and friends. When they suddenly start waking up in each other’s bodies, they initially struggle with the confusion and complications of living each other’s lives, leaving notes and messages to communicate.

As they begin to understand the body-switching phenomenon, Taki and Mitsuha develop a deep connection, forming a bond through the unusual experiences they share. They learn to navigate each other’s lives and help each other with personal challenges. Mitsuha helps Taki improve his social skills and pursue his crush, while Taki helps Mitsuha gain confidence and popularity in her school. Through their communication, they develop a unique closeness, realizing they have grown to care deeply for one another, even though they have never met in person.

One day, however, the body-swapping abruptly stops, leaving Taki desperate to understand what happened to Mitsuha. He begins searching for her, only to discover a shocking truth: Mitsuha’s village, Itomori, was destroyed three years prior by a comet fragment, and she perished along with most of her town. Realizing the complexity of their connection, Taki attempts to reach Mitsuha across time and space, hoping to warn her and save the village. Through sheer determination, he successfully reconnects with her, and the two work together to evacuate the town and prevent the tragic event.

In the end, Taki and Mitsuha are separated once more, their memories of each other fading with time. However, years later, fate brings them together again on a Tokyo street, and they feel an inexplicable pull toward one another. They finally reunite, recognizing the bond they once shared. Your Name is celebrated for its beautiful animation, emotional depth, and exploration of fate, love, and memory, capturing the hearts of audiences worldwide.

Earnings:

Your Name became a massive global success, earning over $380 million worldwide, making it one of the highest-grossing anime films of all time. It set records in Japan and performed exceptionally well internationally, marking Makoto Shinkai as a global name in animation.
